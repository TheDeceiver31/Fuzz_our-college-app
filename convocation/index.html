Prof. Barjor Mehta

Title: President of CEPT University, Ahmedabad, India.

Prof. Barjor Mehta is the President of CEPT University, Ahmedabad, India. The University was established in 1962 and focuses on understanding, designing, planning, constructing, and managing human habitats. The University comprises six faculties: Architecture, Planning, Technology, Design, Management, and a Foundation Program. Prof. Mehta has close to 40 years of experience in the urban sector, including working with the World Bank on various projects globally.

×

Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay

Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay did her B Tech, M Tech, and Ph.D. in Computer Science from Calcutta University, IIT Kharagpur, and the Indian Statistical Institute, respectively. She is currently the Director of the Indian Statistical Institute. Her research interests include computational biology, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. She has received numerous awards including the Padma Shri award in 2022.
